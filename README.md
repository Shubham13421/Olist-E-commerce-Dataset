# Olist-E-commerce-Dataset
# 🛒 Olist E-Commerce Data Analysis (SQL + Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

In this project, I analyzed the Brazilian Olist e-commerce dataset using **SQL and Power BI** to extract meaningful business insights. It focuses on customer behavior, sales performance, and revenue trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and revenue performance
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Evaluate payment methods and customer retention
* Discover trends and seasonality in sales

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **MySQL** (Data Analysis)
* **Power BI** (Data Visualization)
* **SQL** (Joins, Aggregations, Window Functions)

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Basic Analysis

* Total Orders & Customers
* Total Revenue & Average Order Value (AOV)
* Average Items per Order

### 🔹 Customer Analysis

* Top Customers by Spending
* Repeat vs One-time Customers
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV - Basic)
* Customer Retention Rate

### 🔹 Product & Sales Analysis

* Top Products by Revenue
* Category-wise Sales Performance
* Top-selling Product in Each Category

### 🔹 Payment Analysis

* Orders by Payment Method
* Revenue by Payment Type vs Order Count

### 🔹 Advanced SQL Analysis

* Ranking Customers using `RANK()`
* Running Total of Revenue (Window Function)
* Peak Order Day/Month
* Monthly Sales Trend

---

## 📈 Dashboard (Power BI)

The dashboard includes:

* 📈 Monthly Sales Trend (Filtered for meaningful period due to partial data)
* 📊 Top 10 Product Categories by Revenue

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

* ~99K orders generated ~15.8M in total revenue across ~96K customers
* November 2017 recorded peak sales, likely due to seasonal demand
* **Beleza_saude** is the top revenue-generating category
* Boleto is the most used payment method
* Customer retention is very low (~3%), indicating reliance on new customers

---

## ⚠️ Data Note

The dataset contains partial data for some years. Trend analysis was focused on the most complete period to ensure accuracy.
